title: "Stake your first LPT"
description: "Bond LPT to an orchestrator and start earning a share of fees and rewards."
---

<Note>
  This page is a placeholder tutorial. It will walk a delegator through the staking flow end to end.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Acquire LPT">Buy LPT on a supported exchange and move it to a self-custody wallet.</Step>
  <Step title="Choose an orchestrator">Evaluate orchestrators by uptime, reward cut, and fee share.</Step>
  <Step title="Bond and delegate">Submit the bond transaction; tokens become bonded immediately and start earning next round.</Step>
  <Step title="Track rewards">Use the explorer to monitor your delegation's rewards round-by-round.</Step>
</Steps>
